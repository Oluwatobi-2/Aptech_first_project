About Aptech

Aptech, a global leader in education,has trained over 7 million students in 40 countries across 5 continents since 1986. With an updated curriculum, certified faculty and world class infrastructure, Aptech has consistently offered industry-relevant training and education for over 35 years.It has played an integral role in transforming many students into industry ready professionals. A number of international accolades and awards are a testament to this excellence in education.
